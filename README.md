## About This Portfolio

This portfolio showcases five writing assignments completed throughout CIS311: Technical Writing in CIS at Post University. Each document demonstrates my ability to communicate technical information clearly and professionally across a variety of formats — from formal research reports and business proposals to technical instructions, data analysis, and academic abstracts.

## Portfolio Contents

### 1. Locked Out: Examining the Ransomware Threat, Real-World Impacts, and What Organizations Must Do to Stay Protected
**Format:** Formal Research Report
**Description:** A comprehensive, ten-source research report examining how ransomware attacks work, which sectors are most at risk, and what organizations must do to defend themselves. Draws on peer-reviewed journals, government advisories from CISA, and industry publications. Covers the technical mechanics of attacks, the human factor in cybersecurity vulnerabilities, and a layered defense strategy grounded in current research.

### 2. Building Beyond the Bell: A Proposal for a Multiuse Community Campus
**Format:** Internal Memo / Formal Proposal
**Description:** A formal internal memo addressed to a school district superintendent recommending the adoption of a multiuse community campus model for an upcoming construction project. Uses APA 7 citations, real-world case studies, and a cost-feasibility argument to make the case for a facility that serves both students and the broader community.

### 3. How to Connect to a Wi-Fi Network on Windows
**Format:** Technical Instructions / User Guide
**Description:** A step-by-step instructional document guiding users through connecting a Windows 10 or Windows 11 computer to a Wi-Fi network. Written for a non-technical audience, the document includes a process overview diagram, caution and warning callouts, and clear formatting designed to make each step easy to follow.

### 4. Data Mining in Education: Summary and Abstract Analysis
**Format:** Academic Summary and Abstract Writing
**Description:** A structured analysis of a peer-reviewed study on early-alert systems for at-risk college students. Includes a full article summary, a descriptive abstract, an informative abstract, and a reflection comparing both abstract types. Demonstrates the ability to synthesize complex research and present findings accurately and concisely.

### 5. World Orange and Tangerine Production (2017–2021)
**Format:** Data Visualization and Technical Analysis
**Description:** An analytical document presenting FAO production data through an APA 7-formatted table and bar chart. The accompanying paragraph identifies key trends — including Brazil's 57.6% production increase and the United States' near-47% decline — and situates them within broader global shifts in citrus agriculture. Demonstrates the ability to communicate data clearly through both visuals and written analysis.
